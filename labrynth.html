The Enormous Silver Castle

According to legend and rumor, this castle was cursed, and the curse of the labyrinth would never be broken unless the true treasure lying deep within the castle was brought back.

But according to others, the master of the castle was an immensely gorgeous princess, who was delicate as a white thread, and ephemeral as the moon.

That night, a knight stepped foot into this castle swarming with rumors...

«Hey, listen, our esteemed knight is coming!! Quickly, get into position! Archfiend! I told you, you've got Grand Gallery duty tonight!»

«Chandraglier, it is not time to sleep.»

«Phew, barely made it! ...I mean a-ahem! WELCOME, DEAR KNIGHT! Tonight, the staff of the Silver Castle of Labrynth are pleased to present you with the utmost courtesy and respectful hospitality.»

«Come in, please enjoy yourself to the fullest until the very end.»

«First, let us begin with the Reception Hall! It includes a collection of furnishings that Her Highness the Princess has elegantly and elaborately decorated. It is the result of roughly three days' worth of burning the midnight oil.»

«They shall come at you from every direction, from every nook and cranny, so please, enjoy the blood-thirsty, diabolic interior of the castle to your heart's content.»

«Now, let's see how far has that knight gotten by no— hey, that was quick! In front of the staircase already! Augh, we worked soooo hard to prepare!»

«Didn't even take the time to appreciate it. I wonder what I should tell Her Highness the Princess when she inquires as usual about how much our dear knight enjoyed and suffered. Though, our middle elder sister will scold us later for how quickly this was.»

«At least, it's time for the two of us to have some fun.»

«Yes, the two of us will have a chance to inflict some suffering.»

«Oh dear noble knight, how quickly you have arrived! But to move forward, thou shalt face us, the younger sisters of the Ariadne white porcelain maidens: Ariane, and...!»

«...Arianna, for we shall be your opponents.»

Several hours later...

«Phew, got away in the end. I doubt I will be able to move for some time.»

(Chandraglier gurgles and Stovie Torbie whines)

«Oh my, you guys did a great job too. But, I wonder if Her Highness the Princess will be satisfied with this. It probabyl has to do with her being stuck indoor all this time, but she seems awfully excited today.»

(Cuckoo creaks and one of the lamps hisses)

«It is fine, our noble middle older sisters are still waiting in the Grand Garden. Besides, I am sure our dear knight will not be able to find it tonight either. The true treasure remains trapped within this castle... hrm, what is it, Archfiend?»

(Archfiend rumbles)

«Hey, you're supposed to be in the Grand Gallery! If someone goes through there, they end up right in Her Highness' rooms, you know?»

(Archfiend rumbles)

«Huh... 'Her Highness will not wait'?»

«'She just rushed out and disappeared'!? IT'S S-S-SERIOUS!! With the castle being full of traps, it's absolutely not safe!!»
